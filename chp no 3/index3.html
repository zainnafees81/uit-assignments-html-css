Muffin Recipe

A basic Recipe .you can add pretty much anything to it!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 200g caster suger
  • 250ml milk
  • 125ml vegetable oil
  • 400g plan flour

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as ifyou are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
